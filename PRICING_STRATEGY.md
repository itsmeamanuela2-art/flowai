## 📊 Customer Acquisition

### 1. Organic Growth (Best ROI)
- **Google SEO:** "Email automation SaaS", "Workflow automation tool"
  - Timeline: 2-3 Monate für Rankings
  - Cost: €0 (nur Zeit)
  - Expected: 5-10 customers/monat

- **Content Marketing:** Blog Posts, Tutorials
  - Schreibe über: "How to automate emails", "5 automation workflows"
  - Timeline: 1-2 Monate für erste Ergebnisse
  - Expected: 2-5 customers/monat

- **Social Media:** LinkedIn, Twitter
  - Poste regelmäßig über Automation Tips
  - Timeline: Sofort
  - Expected: 1-3 customers/monat

### 2. Paid Ads (Higher Cost)
- **Google Ads:** "automation software"
  - Budget: €500/monat
  - Expected CAC: €50-100 per customer
  - ROI: Positive nach 3 Monate

- **Facebook/Instagram:** "Small business automation"
  - Budget: €300/monat
  - Expected CAC: €30-50 per customer
  - Zielgruppe: Business owners 25-55

### 3. Partnership & Referral
- Partner mit anderen SaaS Produkten
- Affiliate Program (20% commission)
- Referral Bonus für bestehende Kunden

## 💡 Sales Funnel

```
Landing Page Visitors (1000/monat)
         ↓ (5% Click)
Free Sign Ups (50/monat)
         ↓ (20% Visit Pricing)
Pricing Page (10/monat)
         ↓ (50% Try Plan)
Trial/Checkout (5/monat)
         ↓ (80% Convert)
Paying Customers (4-5/monat)
```

**Optimierungsziel:**
- Landing Page → Pricing: 10% → 15%
- Pricing → Checkout: 50% → 75%
- Checkout → Paying: 80% → 90%
